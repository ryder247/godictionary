Go Dictionary of Graphic Design,
Multimedia & Advertising.



Go Dictionary app is an offline dictionary containing terms and their meanings in graphic design, multimedia and advertising industry.

This app will benefit the graphic design industry, animators, illustrated, advertisers, printing industry, multimedia industry ,schools and interested persons in and outside Ghana.
It is imperative for all understudies of graphic design, multimedia , advertising and also related subjects ,as well as those working for the first time in printing, graphic design ,multimedia ,production ,multimedia to have a dictionary. Go dictionary is here to serve this purpose.

Features of the app includes:
•Search a word: This feature allows you to search for the word you want and its meaning.
• Share:This feature allows you to share a download link of the app and words to yours friends
• Random: This feature allows you to select words at random
• Favorites: this feature allows you to store your favorite Words at any time.
• Developers: This feature tells you about the developers
• Rate us: This feature allows you to send your feedbacks, suggestions or comments to the developers. You can also report a problem that may have escaped the developers
•. Help: This feature gives you guidelines and tips to help you understand how the app works
